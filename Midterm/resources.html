Resources

Below are lists of resources that Asian and non-Asian communites can utilize. These resources allow us to support Asian communities during this time of elevated violence, and offer various resources that Asian communites can take advantage of.
  • Monetary donations can be made to the various donation pages provided, including a GoFundMe and six other meaningful organizations.
  • Discrimination has major impacts on one's physical and mental well-being. If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health related to rising anti-Asian hate, explore the different AAPI mental health resources available.
  • Additional resource and awareness pages are provided to learn more about Asian American experiences, hate, and history. These additional pages also provide further opportunities to get involved through donations, organizations, and volunteering.
  • Reporting Anti-Asian discrimination is extremely important. Three websites are listed for you to report discrimination you personally experience or witness someone experience.
  • Get involved on social media! Follow the instagram pages provided to get regular updates on supporting Asian communities and fighting against hate.
