Number of Anti-Asian Incidents in the U.S. by State

Choropleth Map

Hover over a state to view the total amount of anti-Asian hate incidents from March 2020 to March 2021, as reported by The New York Times.

Hate incidents include harassment towards an individual or group, and towards people and establishments. This information does not include all media reports of anti-Asian hate, and is limited to data from one article.

The legend (bottom right) states the relationship between the colors and the amount of hate incidents documented. The hate incidents range from 0 to 42 accounts in each state.
